Release 0: Equip Your New Robot
-From outside the elevator:
	-Determine what floor you are currently on.
	-Select whether you want to go up or down.
		-If you wish to go up, press the upward facing arrow button. That button should now be illuminated. If not, try again.
		-If you wish to go down, press the downward facing arrow button. That button should now be illuminated. If not, try again.
	-Wait for the elevator to reach your floor. Once you hear a ding and your selected button is no longer illuminated, the elevator doors should open.
	-Step inside the elevator.
-When inside the elevator:
	-Select the floor number you desire to go to and press the corresponding numbered button.
	-Elevator should now move in the direction you initially pressed.
	-Once floor is reached, you should hear another ding. Doors will open and you will thus proceed exiting the elevator.


Release 1: Running the Dishwasher
-Gather dirty dishes, utensils, silverware, tupperware, cups, and any other small kitchenware.
-Gently rinse each item in your sink under warm water before beginning to load them into the dishwasher:
	-Load utensils and silverware into the small baskets.
	-Load items such as plates, bowls, and anything that may need more thorough cleaning in the bottom rack.
	-Load cups and more delicate items on the top rack.
	-Insert soap packet or pour soap into designated placeholder usually located on the inside of the door.
	-Close the dishwasher, turn the dial to desired time and settings, and let it run all the way to finish.
-When dishwasher is finished running:
	-Open the door but be careful as items are still very hot.
	-Pull the bottom rack out and begin to organize the clean items:
		-Take each item and place them with others of the same kind in your cupboards and cabinets
	-If some items are not fully clean:
		-Leave them in the dishwasher for cleaning next time.
		-Or take them out and manually clean them in your sink.
